The Best Movies of 2024 According to Me

My top 5 movies of 2024 are:

1. Wild Robot

Wild Robot is a movie about a robot who gets stranded on a deserted island and has to learn how to survive in the wild. It's a heartwarming story about friendship and the power of nature.

2. Inside Out 2

Inside Out 2 is the sequel to the hit Pixar movie Inside Out. It follows the emotions of a teenage girl. The best part of the movie is how the emotion "Anxiety" affects the teenager.

3. Deadpool and Wolverine

Deadpool and Wolverine is a crossover movie featuring two of Marvel's most popular characters. The movie is full of action, humor, and heart, and is a must-see for any superhero fan.

4. Kung Fu Panda 4

Po is gearing up to become the spiritual leader of his Valley of Peace, but also needs someone to take his place as Dragon Warrior. As such, he will train a new kung fu practitioner for the spot and will encounter a villain called the Chameleon who conjures villains from the past.

5. Despicable Me 4

Despicable Me 4 is the fourth installment in the popular animated movie series. The movie follows Gru and his family as they face off against a new villain who threatens to destroy the world. It's a fun and heartwarming movie that's perfect for the whole family.
